## Introduction

Mānoa CourseWise is a student-driven platform that helps UH Mānoa students make informed decisions about their course selections. Here's what you can do on the site:

- **Browse courses** and see aggregated ratings and reviews
- **Search for courses** by course code (e.g., ICS 311, MATH 242)
- **Read reviews** from other students about course difficulty, workload, and clarity
- **Submit your own reviews** to help future students
- **Track your submitted reviews** in your personal dashboard

### Key Statistics
- 1,847+ Courses Reviewed
- 12,459+ Student Reviews
- 4.6 Average Rating
- 87% Would Take Again

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click the **Login** button in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Sign Up** at the bottom of the sign-in form
3. Enter your:
   - Email address
   - Password (must meet security requirements)
4. Click **Sign Up** to create your account
5. You'll receive a confirmation and be logged in automatically

### Signing In

1. Click **Login** in the top navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### User Roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Guest** | Can browse courses and read reviews |
| **User** | Can also submit reviews and access personal dashboard |
| **Admin** | Full access including user and content management |

## Browsing Courses

### Using the Search Bar

The main search bar on the landing page allows you to quickly find courses by:
- Course code (e.g., "ICS 311")
- Course name (e.g., "Algorithms")
- Professor name

### Course Search Page

1. Click **Search Courses** in the navigation bar
2. You'll see a list of available courses with:
   - Course code and title
   - Lead professor name
   - Average rating (0-5 stars)
   - Number of reviews
   - Difficulty, Workload, and Clarity ratings (1-5)
   - Top tags (e.g., "Homework heavy", "Challenging")

3. Click on any course card to view full details

## Viewing Course Details

When you click on a course, you'll see a detailed page showing:

### Course Information
- Course code and full name
- All professors who teach the course

### Ratings Overview
- Overall average rating
- Total number of reviews
- Average scores for:
  - **Difficulty** (1-5)
  - **Workload** (1-5)
  - **Clarity** (1-5)

### Tags
- Most commonly used tags for the course
- Tags include: "Homework heavy", "Lecture heavy", "Exam heavy", "Group projects", "Challenging", "Easy grading"

### Reviews
- Full list of student reviews (most recent first)
- Each review shows:
  - Rating (stars)
  - Review text
  - Semester taken
  - Whether the review is anonymous
  - Tags associated with the review

## Submitting a Review

> **Note:** You must be logged in to submit a review.

### Step-by-Step Process

1. Click **Submit Review** in the navigation bar (or click the button on the landing page)
2. Fill out the review form:

#### Select Course & Professor
- Choose a course from the dropdown
- Choose the professor from the dropdown (options update based on selected course)

#### Semester Information
- Select the semester when you took the course
- Options range from Fall 2023 to Spring 2026

#### Ratings (1-5 scale)
- **Overall Rating**: Your overall impression
- **Difficulty**: How challenging the course was
- **Workload**: Amount of work required
- **Clarity**: How well the material was explained

#### Written Review
- Write your detailed review in the text area
- This field is required

#### Tags
- Select one or more tags that describe the course:
  - Homework heavy
  - Lecture heavy
  - Exam heavy
  - Group projects
  - Challenging
  - Easy grading

#### Anonymous Option
- Check the box if you want your review to be anonymous
- Anonymous reviews won't show your email

3. Click **Submit Review**
4. You'll see a success notification, and your review will be added to the course page

## FAQ

### How do I find a specific course?

Use the search bar on the landing page, or navigate to Search Courses and browse the list. You can search by course code, name, or professor.

### Can I submit a review without an account?

No, you must create an account and be logged in to submit reviews. This helps maintain review quality and accountability.

### How are ratings calculated?

Ratings are the average of all student submissions. Each review includes ratings for overall quality, difficulty, workload, and clarity.

### Can I edit or delete my submitted reviews?

Currently, you can view your reviews in your dashboard, but editing/deleting functionality may be limited. Contact an administrator if you need assistance.

### What do the tags mean?

Tags are community-generated labels that describe course characteristics:
- **Homework heavy**: Lots of homework assignments
- **Lecture heavy**: Focus on lectures
- **Exam heavy**: Many exams
- **Group projects**: Group work required
- **Challenging**: Difficult material
- **Easy grading**: Generous grading

### How do I report an inappropriate review?

Contact the site administrator through the admin panel or by reaching out directly. Admins can manage and remove inappropriate content.

### Is my review anonymous?

You can choose to make your review anonymous by checking the anonymous option. Anonymous reviews won't display your email address.
